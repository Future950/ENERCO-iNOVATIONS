About Enerco Innovations

How It Started, and Why

Enerco Innovations was born from a vision to transform Nigeria's energy landscape, making reliable and sustainable power accessible to every small business.

What We Want to Achieve

We envision a future where Nigeria's economic landscape is powered by a decentralized, dependable, and clean energy grid, enabling small businesses to thrive and contribute to a healthier planet.

How We Will Achieve It

To make sustainable energy accessible to every small business in Nigeria by acting as a trusted partner, providing flexible financing for reliable solutions, and managing the entire process from start to finish.

What We Stand For

Integrity and Trust

We build relationships on a foundation of honesty and transparency, always acting with the highest integrity to earn the trust of our customers, partners, and team.

Inclusion and Community

We are committed to fostering equity by providing accessible energy solutions for everyone. We believe in the power of community and strive to create a more prosperous future where all businesses have the opportunity to succeed.

Innovation and Impact

We relentlessly pursue innovative solutions that deliver tangible results. Our purpose is to create a positive impact by empowering our customers to grow while contributing to a cleaner, more sustainable environment.
